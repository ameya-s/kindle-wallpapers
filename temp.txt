Lest I forget 

I have been reading books on kindle over the years and highlighting interesting parts. but seldom could I revisit all those highlights.

Knowledge from a book has a half life, each re-read slows the decay. 

Highlighting important parts efficiently curates the essence of the material.

but the interface to go back is just too many steps. so it never happens.

Why not just show them as wallpaper. computer wallpaper is the easiest interface. highlights just show up through the day, no effort.

then I built a small thing that pulls all highlights from kindle, makes a wallpaper per highlight, mac rotates through them.

a lot of good stuff was just sitting there. now it isn't.
